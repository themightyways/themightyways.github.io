Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need any equipment to start?

No. We bring mats and training gear. Comfortable clothing and water are enough for the first session.

Where do classes take place?

We are mobile across Henderson and Las Vegas: schools, parks, community centers, HOAs, private homes and events.

Is there a contract or long‑term commitment?

No long‑term contracts. Start with once a week; increase frequency as you go.

Can we host a one‑time class (e.g., birthday or festival)?

Yes! We offer turnkey single‑event classes and demos. Tell us your date and venue — we’ll handle the rest.
